Mental Health in Workplaces

The Problem

In a survey addressing mental health in a tech workplace, 71% of the people said resources were NOT provided to learn about mental health and to seek help. Why is this? This is because mental health is disregarded in comparison to physical health.

The number of suicides in workplace environment are increasing becaue of the lack of resources available for people facing mental health issues.
