Workplace Suicides Trends over Time

Over time, there has been a slowly growing trend of suicides due to the workplace environment. Solely in the year 2013, there were 282 workplace suicides. From 1992 to 2013, workplace suicides have increased by 38% in just 21 years.

Has your employer ever discussed mental health as part of an employee wellness program?

In a survey studying mental health in technology-based workplaces, the donut chart displays that 66.9% of employees do not have mental health resources as part of their wellness program. 18.2% having mental health resources with their wellness program and 14.9% unsure if their company provides mental health resources with their wellness program.

Does your employer provide mental health benefits?

Amongst 1,260 tech employees, 37.9% of these employees are provided with mental health benefits yet 29.7% are not and 32.4% are unsure whether or not they are provided with such benefits.

Do you know the options for mental health care your employer provides?

This donut chart displays the ratio of whether or not tech employees are aware of their mental health care options. 39.8% of these employees do not know the options their employer provides, with 35.3% knowing their options, and 24.9% being unsure of whether or not they know the options their employer provides.

Does your employer provide resources to learn more about mental health issues and how to seek help?

Many companies fail to inform their employees of the depth of their wellness programs. As seen before, 66.9% of tech employees have never been discussed with about mental health resources as part of their wellness program. With this, 51.3% of tech employees are not provided the resources to learn more about mental health.
